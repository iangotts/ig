The Voice

She came up through musical theatre, where you learn to carry a song all the way to the back row without ever losing the person in the front.

That training is what you hear the moment Marti steps to the microphone: real power held with real warmth, phrasing that lands every lyric, and the ease of a performer who reads a room before she sings a note.

For a cocktail hour she keeps it intimate and golden-lit. For the big moment she opens up and fills the space. Either way, she sets the tone for the whole night — and guests remember the music.
